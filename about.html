David Michael Deloso

Journalism, but on the Internet!

I’m a third-year studying journalism, human-centered design and Asian American Studies at Northwestern University. My focus is on building immersive online stories for journalism using code.

I’m currently an Interactives Editor at North by Northwestern, one of the largest student publications on campus. There, I'm helping lead a team of journalists and developers as they push the boundaries of student journalism. In addition to my work with Interactives, I've amassed a wide range of skills in multimedia journalism — I've taken photos of concerts, shot and edited videos, produced and co-hosed a podcast and designed graphics using Illustrator. Previous positions at NBN include Editor-in-chief and Entertainment Editor.

Beyond journalism, I’m interested in exploring media innovation through human-computer interaction. I'm always looking for ways to go beyond the scope of traditional forms of storytelling and create new experiences for readers. Let’s push the boundaries together!
