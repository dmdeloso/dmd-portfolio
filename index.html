nuazn.com | August 2020

NU Asian Magazine (stylized as NUAZN) is Northwestern's only Asian-interest publication. During Summer 2020, I joined staff as webmaster and developed the magazine's website. All HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP was written by me, with WordPress being used as the site's content management system.

First contact | Sept. 2020

How do you start college remotely? I spoke to incoming Northwestern students about their experience finding community virtually after the university announced most of them would not be allowed on campus. I built the interactive components over the course of a week, using JavaScript and JQuery to simulate an email notification and a text group chat for dramatic effect.

Romanticizing NU Sports | Oct. 2020

Which iconic moment from Northwestern sports history are you? Swipe through the bios of various Northwestern stereotypes to find out! For a collaboration between North by Northwestern's Life & Style and Sports sections, I developed a graphic that replicates the interface of a popular dating app to build a quiz with various outcomes! All HTML, CSS and JavaScript was written by me.

Dillo Day 2019 Artist Guide | May 2019

Dillo Day, held annualy at Northwestern, is the largest student-run music festival in the country, featuring a lineup of famous artists on the main stage. As entertainment editor for North by Northwestern, I created an interactive guide to the mainstage artists. All web development was done by me, and artist write-ups were contributed by various entertainment writers
